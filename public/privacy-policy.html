Privacy Policy for MatchDay Coach

Last updated: August 2025
Operated by Velomo

1. Who We Are

MatchDay Coach is operated by Velomo, a company based in Finland. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Contact Information:
Email: support@matchdaycoach.com
Business: Velomo, Finland

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Email address – Required to create and access your account.
  • Optional player names – Entered by users to label and track team data.
  • Game data – Match information, scores, events, and statistics you create.
  • IP addresses – May be logged automatically by our service providers for security and hosting purposes.
  • Device and browser information – Collected via analytics to understand general usage patterns and improve app performance.

We do not collect or store sensitive personal information such as payment details, biometric data, or precise location information.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the MatchDay Coach service, including:

  • Authenticating user accounts and maintaining secure access
  • Saving and managing game data and team information
  • Allowing users to export and delete their data
  • Providing customer support when requested
  • Improving app functionality and user experience through aggregated analytics

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or advertising purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the basis of:

  • Contract – To provide you with access to the app and its features.
  • Legitimate interest – For app security, functionality, and minimal analytics to improve the service.
  • Consent – For optional features like analytics, which you can disable in settings.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored using:

  • Supabase (for database and authentication) - hosted in EU/US regions
  • Vercel (for frontend hosting) - global CDN with EU/US presence

Data may be stored in the EU or other regions depending on the infrastructure configuration. We implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

Retention Policy: When you delete your account, we permanently remove your personal data within 30 days. Some metadata may remain temporarily in provider-level backups for up to 90 days for technical recovery purposes only.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies:

  • Essential cookies – Required for login and app functionality (authentication tokens, session management)
  • Analytics cookies – Optional usage analytics that can be disabled in app settings
  • Performance cookies – To monitor app performance and identify issues

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking for marketing purposes.

7. User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the app's export feature
  • Rectify incorrect data by editing it directly in the app
  • Export your data in standard formats (JSON, CSV) from the app
  • Delete your account and all related data directly from the app settings
  • Portability – Download your data to transfer to another service
  • Object to processing by contacting us directly
  • Contact us regarding any privacy concerns

Children's Data: We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13. If player names entered identify minors, the user is responsible for ensuring they have proper permission to enter that information.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – Authentication and database (may log IPs and set session cookies)
  • Vercel – Hosting and analytics (may log IPs and device info for performance)
  • App Stores – Google Play Store and Apple App Store for distribution (we do not access billing info)

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties. Each service provider has their own privacy policy governing their data practices.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication and session management
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and user data isolation

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no internet-based service can guarantee 100% security.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the European Economic Area, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Using service providers that comply with GDPR adequacy decisions
  • Implementing Standard Contractual Clauses where necessary
  • Ensuring equivalent levels of data protection

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if our services or legal obligations change. Significant changes will be communicated through the app with at least 30 days notice. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
